What Tend Is

Tend is a managed AI operator consultancy.

We design, deploy, run, and support AI-powered workflow operators for businesses. Not chatbots. Not dashboards. Controlled systems that handle real operational work with human oversight built in.

The Positioning

  • What we areManaged AI integration service
  • What we're notChatbots, dashboards, or DIY tools
  • How we're differentWe build, run, and maintain it for you
  • Parent companyKyros

The Problem We Solve

Most businesses know AI can help. Few know how to deploy it safely inside real workflows.

The gap is not technology. The gap is implementation: scoping the right workflow, setting up proper controls, connecting to existing tools, and keeping it running reliably over time.

That gap is expensive. Teams either waste months experimenting or overpay for enterprise solutions they don't need yet.

What We Sell

Entry Offer: One Agent, One Workflow, Managed

We start with a single, tightly scoped deployment:

  1. Discovery: Identify the highest-value workflow to automate
  2. Design: Map permissions, escalation logic, and success criteria
  3. Deploy: Build and launch the operator in the client's environment
  4. Run: Monitor, tune, and support on an ongoing basis

This is intentionally narrow. One workflow, proven ROI, then expand.

Expansion: Multi-Agent Systems

Once the first agent proves value, we layer in additional operators:

  • Orchestrator coordinating specialist agents
  • Cross-workflow automation
  • Broader tool integrations
  • Higher support tiers

We earn the right to expand. We don't sell complexity upfront.

Delivery Methodology

  1. Select the workflow — Find the pain point with clear inputs, outputs, and enough value to justify the investment.
  2. Scope the operator — Define what it does, what it doesn't, what tools it touches, and what needs human approval.
  3. Deploy the system — Set up the channel, context, integrations, permissions, and operating rules.
  4. Run with oversight — Monitor, support, tune, and handle exceptions. Monthly review loops with the client.
  5. Expand after proof — Add scope, specialist operators, and orchestration once the first deployment has demonstrated clear value.

Competitive Position

Most AI consultancies sell one of two things: generic chatbot deployments or bloated enterprise transformation programs.

Tend sits in the middle.

  • More operational than chatbot shops — We deploy into real workflows with real permissions, not just customer-facing chat windows
  • More accessible than enterprise AI firms — No 6-figure contracts, no 6-month timelines, no procurement theater
  • Managed, not abandoned — We don't build and walk away. Ongoing operation is the business model

The moat is execution speed and operational depth, not proprietary technology.

Trust and Control

Every deployment includes:

  • Scoped access — Operators only touch what they're authorized to touch
  • Approval gates — High-stakes actions require human sign-off
  • Human oversight — Monitoring and exception handling are built into the service
  • Audit-friendly operations — Actions are logged and reviewable
  • Managed deployment — Tend operates and maintains the system

We are intentionally conservative about claims. We do not promise enterprise compliance certification or fully autonomous operations.
