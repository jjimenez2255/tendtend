## The Full Explanation (2 minutes)

Every growing business hits the same wall. You've got 3 problems:

**1. Too much admin, not enough doers.** Your best people spend half their day on scheduling, follow-ups, data entry, and email. Work that matters but doesn't move the needle.

**2. Things fall through the cracks.** Leads don't get called back. Appointments don't get confirmed. Follow-ups happen 3 days late. Not because anyone's lazy — because there's too much to track.

**3. Hiring more people doesn't scale.** A new hire costs $40-60K/year, takes weeks to train, calls in sick, and might quit. You need the capacity without the headcount.

**That's where we come in.**

We build a custom AI agent — think of it as a virtual team member — that handles your repetitive operations. It connects to your calendar, your email, your CRM. It follows your processes, uses your tone of voice, and knows your pricing.

**What it actually does:**
- Responds to leads within minutes (not hours)
- Books appointments and sends confirmations
- Follows up automatically at the right intervals
- Prepares meeting briefs so you walk into calls informed
- Sends weekly reports on what happened
- Escalates anything it can't handle to a human

**What makes us different from chatbots:**
- A chatbot follows a script. It breaks when someone asks something unexpected.
- Our agents understand context, adapt to conversations, and handle complexity.
- They're not bolted onto your website — they're integrated into your operations.

**The economics:**
- Setup: $3,000-8,000 (one-time, based on complexity)
- Monthly: $500-1,500 (ongoing support, monitoring, improvements)
- vs. hiring: A part-time admin runs $2,000-3,000/month minimum
- ROI typically hits within 30-60 days

## How It Works (The Process)

1. **Discovery Call** (30 min) — We learn your business, your pain points, your tools, your workflow. Recorded with Fathom for accuracy.

2. **Proposal** (within 48 hours) — Custom scope, specific to what we discussed. No generic packages.

3. **Build** (1-2 weeks) — We configure the agent: identity, integrations, processes, escalation rules. We test 20+ scenarios.

4. **Handoff** (45 min) — Live demo with you. We walk through every feature. You approve before it goes live.

5. **Support** (ongoing) — We monitor daily for the first month, weekly after. Monthly strategy call to review performance and optimize.

## Key Differentiators (Memorize These)

1. **Dedicated, not generic.** Your agent is built for your business. It knows your pricing, your processes, your clients' names.

2. **Operator, not tool.** It doesn't just connect things — it does the work. Writes emails, books appointments, follows up, escalates.

3. **We eat our own cooking.** Our COO is an AI agent. If it's good enough to run our business, it's good enough for yours.

4. **Experiment-driven.** We run continuous experiments on your outreach, your messaging, your processes. What works stays. What doesn't gets killed. You get compounding improvements, not a static tool.

5. **Human guardrails.** The agent has clear boundaries. Important decisions escalate to you. You're always in control.
