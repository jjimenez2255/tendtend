## Objection Handling

### "Why can't I just use ChatGPT?"
> "You can. But having a tool isn't the same as having a system. ChatGPT doesn't connect to your CRM, respond to leads at 2am, or know your escalation rules. We build the system that does."

### "Will AI replace my employees?"
> "No — this is employee amplification, not replacement. Your team focuses on high-value work. The AI handles the repetitive stuff they hate anyway."

### "What if it makes mistakes?"
> "It will, at first. That's why we have human-in-the-loop checkpoints for anything high-stakes. And we're monitoring and tuning constantly. Mistakes get caught and fixed fast."

### "Is my data safe?"
> "Yes. Scoped permissions — the AI only touches what it needs. We don't train on your data. Actions are logged and auditable. You own everything."

### "I'm not technical. Can I use this?"
> "That's the whole point. We handle the tech. You describe how your business works. We make it happen."

### "This seems expensive."
> "Compare it to hiring. A full-time admin is $40K+ with benefits, works 40 hours/week, calls in sick. Tend is $24K/year, works 24/7, never misses a lead. The math works."

### "I've tried AI tools before and they didn't work."
> "Most AI tools are self-serve — you're on your own to figure it out. We're the opposite. We build it, we run it, we fix it. You just see the results."
