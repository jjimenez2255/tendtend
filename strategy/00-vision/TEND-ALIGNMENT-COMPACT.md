## The One Line

> **Tend builds, runs, and supports managed AI operators for business workflows.**

We are not a SaaS platform. We are not selling chatbots. We identify one painful workflow, deploy a controlled AI operator into it, and keep it running.

---

## What We Sell

### Entry: One Operator. One Workflow. Managed.

- Lead qualification and routing
- Meeting prep and follow-up
- Support triage
- Internal ops coordination
- Reporting and status synthesis

### Expansion: Multi-Operator System

After the first operator proves value. Not before.

## Methodology

1. **Select** — Find a workflow with pain, repetition, clear inputs/outputs
2. **Scope** — Define what the operator does, doesn't do, and what needs human approval
3. **Deploy** — Channel, context, integrations, permissions, escalation logic
4. **Run** — Monitor, support, tune, review, handle exceptions
5. **Expand** — Only after proof of value

---

## Target Market

**Go after:** SMBs with budget and workflow pain. Mid-market ops teams. Overloaded founders.

**Avoid:** Tiny budgets. Heavy enterprise procurement. Magic-seekers.

- Consultancy is the primary revenue play
