Questions

How is this different from Instantly or Smartlead?

Those tools send emails. We install an AI that writes them. Every email is personalized based on actual prospect research — not mail merge fields. The AI also handles follow-ups and objection responses.

Do I need to approve every email?

That's up to you. Most clients review the first batch, then let the system run once they're confident in the output. You always have an approval dashboard if you want it.

Will this get my domain blacklisted?

No. We set up proper email infrastructure — dedicated sending domains, warm-up sequences, and volume limits. Deliverability is built into the system.

What if I already have a CRM?

We integrate with it. HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, or even a simple spreadsheet. Leads flow directly into your existing workflow.

How fast can this be set up?

Most installs take 5-7 business days. You'll have your first campaigns running within two weeks of our strategy call.
