FAQ

Common questions

What exactly does Tend do? +
It's a physical AI system installed in your office that automates repetitive business operations — email responses, scheduling, lead follow-up, data entry, invoicing, customer support. Whatever's eating your time and not generating revenue.
Do I need any technical knowledge? +
No. We handle everything — install, setup, configuration, integrations. You continue running your business as normal. The system works in the background.
How is this different from ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT is a chat window you manually use. Tend is an always-on system that acts on its own — monitoring inboxes, responding to leads, executing tasks. It's the difference between having a search engine and having an employee.
What kind of ROI can I expect? +
Most businesses recover the setup cost within 30 days through time saved alone. At $5,000/month, you're paying less than a single hour of a part-time employee per week for 24/7 automation.
What happens if something breaks? +
Your monthly service includes priority support and system health monitoring. We catch issues before you do, and if anything needs attention, we handle it remotely or on-site.
Is my data secure? +
Your data lives on hardware in your office — not on a shared cloud server. You control it entirely. Nothing leaves your network unless you configure it to.
