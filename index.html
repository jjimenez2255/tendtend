What exactly does Tend do? +
We build and run a custom AI operator for your business. It handles repetitive operations — email responses, scheduling, lead follow-up, invoicing, customer support. Whatever's eating your time and not generating revenue.
Do I need any technical knowledge? +
No. We handle everything — discovery, build, integration, and ongoing support. You continue running your business as normal. The operator works in the background.
How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools? +
ChatGPT is a chat window you manually use. We build an always-on operator that acts on its own — monitoring inboxes, responding to leads, executing tasks. It's the difference between having a search engine and having an employee.
Do you use this yourselves? +
Yes. Our COO is an AI agent named Alfred. He tracks our pipeline, monitors our ads, preps meeting briefs, and sends us a morning update every day. We don't sell anything we don't use ourselves.
What kind of ROI can I expect? +
Most businesses recover the setup cost within 30 days through time saved alone. At $2,000/month, you're paying a fraction of what you'd spend on a part-time employee — for 24/7 automation that never makes mistakes.
What if I want to cancel? +
We require a 3-month minimum commitment. After that, you can cancel monthly support anytime — but we never take your operator down. You own what you paid for.
