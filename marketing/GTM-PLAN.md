## Executive Summary

Clawdbot DFW is positioned to capture an emerging market: small businesses seeking practical, tangible AI solutions without the complexity of cloud subscriptions or technical overhead. The DFW metro (7.81M population, $87,155 median household income) has a thriving SMB ecosystem with strong demand for automation tools. This plan outlines a phased approach to market entry, starting with high-intent digital channels and expanding into local business networks.

**Key Insight:** The Dallas Regional Chamber is actively promoting AI adoption with "AI Office Hours" for businesses—this signals strong market interest and potential partnership opportunities.

- **Message:** "Your AI nurtures leads 24/7 so you can close more deals."
- **Targeting:** Realtors, brokerages, property managers
- **Where they are:** Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook

#### 3. **Medical/Dental Offices**
- **Pain:** No-shows, appointment reminders, phone overwhelm
- **Message:** "Reduce no-shows by 40%. Your AI confirms, reminds, and reschedules."
- **Targeting:** Dentists, chiropractors, med spas, private practices

#### 4. **Law Firms**
- **Pain:** Client intake, document requests, billable hour focus
